What will the Earth look like in 200 years?

By 2223, Planet Earth has experienced a remarkable transformation in terms of environmental health. Sustainable practices and innovations in clean energy have led to a nearly carbon-neutral global economy. The world has transitioned to advanced forms of renewable energy, including fusion power, and carbon capture technologies are actively removing excess greenhouse gases from the atmosphere.

Technological advances in 2223 have reached astounding heights. Quantum computing has revolutionized information processing, enabling breakthroughs in areas such as weather prediction, materials science, and healthcare research. Artificial intelligence has become deeply integrated into daily life, optimizing transportation, healthcare diagnostics, and even creative endeavors like art and music.
