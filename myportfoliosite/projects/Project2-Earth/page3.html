What will the Earth look like in 500 years?

In 2523, Earth's environmental health has reached a state of unparalleled harmony. A fully sustainable global economy relies on advanced fusion power, harnessing clean and virtually limitless energy. Carbon dioxide levels in the atmosphere have been reduced to pre-industrial levels through the widespread use of carbon capture and utilization technologies.

Technological advances in 2523 are beyond anything imagined in earlier centuries. Quantum computing has unlocked the secrets of the universe, enabling humanity to explore the farthest reaches of space with unprecedented precision. Artificial general intelligence (AGI) has become a reality, ushering in an era of coexistence and collaboration between humans and sentient machines. Space colonization has expanded to distant exoplanets, and wormhole technology allows for rapid interstellar travel.
