What will the Earth look like in 100 years?

In 2123, Planet Earth exhibits signs of significant environmental recovery and stabilization. Efforts to combat climate change and preserve biodiversity have paid off, with renewable energy sources dominating the global energy landscape. Carbon emissions have been drastically reduced, and large-scale reforestation projects have helped restore natural ecosystems. The world's oceans have started to recover from overfishing and pollution, fostering the revival of marine life.

Technological advances in 2123 have ushered in an era of innovation and interconnectedness. Renewable energy sources, such as advanced solar and wind technologies, have become ubiquitous, leading to a significant decrease in greenhouse gas emissions. Artificial intelligence and automation have revolutionized industries, enhancing productivity and reducing the need for human labor in mundane tasks.
